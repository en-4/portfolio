ABOUT

I work well at optimizing the presentation of visual material for social media, web pages, etc. and have experience in video editing and some voice-over work. I enjoy this type of work a lot, because I am also an individual that is entertained by media. I find myself listening to the specific details in a song, whether it be different instruments, or vocals, or even the absence of sound, seeing and hearing what brings it all together. It is a complex arrangement that produces a good sound, without being fully appreciated. That is how many art forms, not only music, but videos, voice-overs, pictures, all have importance to me in my daily life.
